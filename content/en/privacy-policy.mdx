<h2>2. What data we collect</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Browsing data</strong>: pages viewed, UI events, device/browser type (audience measurement). <em>Collected only after your Analytics consent.</em></li>
<li><strong>Newsletter form</strong>: email address (required). Anti-bot honeypot, anti-bot delay and optional reCAPTCHA v3 protection.</li>
<li><strong>Cookie consent log</strong>: your opt-in/opt-out choices, timestamp, policy version (stored client-side in <code>localStorage</code>).</li>
<li><strong>Technical logs</strong>: data required for security and operations (hosting provider Netlify: source IP, user-agent, response codes).</li>
</ul>
<h2>3. Purposes &amp; legal bases (GDPR)</h2>
<ul className="table-like">
<li><strong>Audience measurement (GA4)</strong> — <em>Legal basis: consent (Art.6-1-a)</em>. Loaded only after you click “Accept all” or save preferences with “Analytics” on.</li>
<li><strong>Newsletter</strong> — <em>Legal basis: consent (Art.6-1-a)</em>. You can unsubscribe anytime via the link in every email.</li>
<li><strong>Affiliate links (sponsored links)</strong> — <em>Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art.6-1-f)</em>. Some outbound links (e.g., TradingView, Bitpanda) may be affiliate links.</li>
<li><strong>Security &amp; operations</strong> (CSP, HSTS, anti-bot, technical logging) — <em>Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art.6-1-f)</em> &amp; legal obligation (Art.6-1-c).</li>
<li><strong>Data subject requests</strong> — <em>Legal basis: legal obligation (Art.6-1-c)</em>.</li>
</ul>
