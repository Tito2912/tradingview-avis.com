Privacy policy

Last updated: September 11, 2025

1. Who we are

TradingView-Avis.com is an independent editorial website presenting the TradingView platform and publishing educational content. Main contact: contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com. This site is neither affiliated with, endorsed by, nor operated by TradingView, Inc.

2. What data we collect

  • Browsing data: pages viewed, UI events, device/browser type (audience measurement). Collected only after your Analytics consent.
  • Newsletter form: email address (required). Anti-bot honeypot, anti-bot delay and optional reCAPTCHA v3 protection.
  • Cookie consent log: your opt-in/opt-out choices, timestamp, policy version (stored client-side in localStorage).
  • Technical logs: data required for security and operations (hosting provider Netlify: source IP, user-agent, response codes).

3. Purposes & legal bases (GDPR)

  • Audience measurement (GA4) — Legal basis: consent (Art.6-1-a). Loaded only after you click “Accept all” or save preferences with “Analytics” on.
  • Newsletter — Legal basis: consent (Art.6-1-a). You can unsubscribe anytime via the link in every email.
  • Affiliate links (sponsored links) — Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art.6-1-f). Some outbound links (e.g., TradingView, Bitpanda) may be affiliate links.
  • Security & operations (CSP, HSTS, anti-bot, technical logging) — Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art.6-1-f) & legal obligation (Art.6-1-c).
  • Data subject requests — Legal basis: legal obligation (Art.6-1-c).

4. Cookies & similar technologies

We use two categories: Necessary (always on) and Analytics (subject to your consent).

NameCategoryPurposeDurationProvider
csp, hsts, cache headers Necessary Security/integrity, encrypted transport Browser-dependent TradingView-Avis.com / Netlify
tv_consent (localStorage) Necessary Consent log (choices, date, version) 13 months TradingView-Avis.com
_ga, _ga_* Analytics GA4 audience measurement (if accepted) 13 months (cookies) / 14 months (event retention) Google Ireland Ltd
_GRECAPTCHA Necessary reCAPTCHA v3 anti-abuse protection Up to 6 months Google Ireland Ltd

By default, the Analytics option is shown as checked in the UI, but no GA4 script is loaded until you explicitly click “Accept all” (opt-in). You can change your choice anytime via the “Cookie preferences” link (in the footer) when available.

5. Recipients & processors

  • Hosting: Netlify, Inc. (USA) — static hosting, technical logs.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (Google Ireland Ltd) — audience measurement, conditionally loaded after consent.
  • Forms: Google Apps Script & Google Sheets (Google Ireland Ltd) — email storage for the newsletter.
  • Form security: reCAPTCHA v3 (Google) — automated abuse prevention.
  • Translation: Google Translate may be loaded on some pages (legal pages block translation).

6. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA (notably in the United States). When applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework where a provider is certified, and/or Standard Contractual Clauses). See each provider’s policy for details.

7. Retention

  • Consent log: 13 months.
  • Analytics cookies: 13 months (browser settings); GA4 events: 14 months.
  • Newsletter: until you unsubscribe or up to 3 years after the last measurable interaction.
  • Technical logs (hosting): per Netlify’s policy (typically a few weeks).

8. Your rights

You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, withdrawal of consent and portability (as per GDPR Articles 15–22). To exercise your rights: contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com.

You can also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in France: cnil.fr).

9. Security

The site implements reasonable technical and organizational measures: TLS encryption, a strict CSP, HSTS, Referrer-Policy, a minimal Permissions-Policy, anti-sniffing headers, and limiting third-party scripts to the bare essentials (YouTube, Google Analytics/Tag, Translate, gstatic, Apps Script).

10. reCAPTCHA v3

Forms may be protected by reCAPTCHA v3. Its use is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

11. Newsletter

By subscribing, you agree to receive informational emails. Your email is securely stored in a Google Sheet via a dedicated Apps Script. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of our emails.

12. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or in the legal framework. In case of material changes, a notice may be displayed on the site. The update date is shown at the top of this page.

13. Contact

For any question regarding this policy, email contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com.
