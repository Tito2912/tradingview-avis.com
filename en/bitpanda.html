Stocks & ETFs: what “Real Securities” implies

Market & hours

Bitpanda states orders are executed on a regulated exchange, with market hours (Mon–Fri 07:30–23:00, not 24/7).

Spread & execution

Bitpanda states it does not charge commissions/overnight fees, but a spread (bid/ask) can apply and vary by trade size and market conditions.

In practice, prices can move between preview and execution (liquidity/market move). This is normal market behaviour.

Security & account: practical points

What Bitpanda highlights

  • Mostly offline storage (cold storage) of its crypto reserve.
  • Session/device management and email confirmations for sensitive actions.
  • DDoS protection and SSL encryption (per Bitpanda).

User best practices

  • Enable 2FA right after signup.
  • Unique password + secure email (ideally email 2FA too).
  • Anti-phishing habits: type the URL, ignore unsolicited “support”.

Exact measures depend on products and country. Check Bitpanda help pages for details.

Bitpanda FAQ

Is Bitpanda suitable for beginners?

The interface is generally accessible, but crypto assets remain risky. Start with an amount that fits your profile and keep learning.

What are the risks of crypto assets?

Volatility can lead to rapid losses. Only invest what you can afford to lose.

What fees should I expect on crypto?

Bitpanda states the cost is shown before confirmation. The stated fee range per trade is 0.00% to 2.49% depending on the asset (e.g., Bitcoin 0.99% buy & sell). Check the summary before confirming.

What is the minimum amount to start?

Bitpanda states a minimum of €10 (or equivalent) for fiat deposits/withdrawals and €1 (or equivalent) for crypto buy/sell/swap.

Does Bitpanda offer stocks and ETFs?

Yes, via Real Stocks & ETFs (Real Securities) depending on your country. Bitpanda states market hours Mon–Fri 07:30–23:00 (not 24/7) and that a spread (bid/ask) can apply.

How does the Bitpanda Card work and what fees apply?

Bitpanda states ATM withdrawals cost 2% of the withdrawal (minimum €2). A payment triggers a conversion (trade) with the usual trading fee. Check limits and settings in the app.

Do I need identity verification (KYC)?

Depending on regulation and the services used, identity verification (KYC) may be required.

Is Bitpanda available in my country?

Availability and the offering vary by country. Bitpanda also states that people with US citizenship or located in the United States may not become users (regulatory reasons).

How can I secure my account?

Enable 2FA, use a unique password and review sessions/devices. Bitpanda also states it stores almost all of its crypto reserve offline (cold storage) and uses email confirmations for sensitive actions.
