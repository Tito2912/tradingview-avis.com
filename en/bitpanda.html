Before you sign up: quick checklist

Security

  • Enable 2FA right after signup.
  • Use a unique password (ideally with a password manager).
  • Review security settings (alerts, email, phone) and watch for phishing.

Fees

  • Check the total cost before confirming (spreads/fees may apply).
  • Review deposit/withdrawal fees based on your payment method.
  • Account for FX conversions if your currency differs.
